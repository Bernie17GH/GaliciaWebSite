🌿 Gardens
Pazo de Oca
~1.4 km · Oca, A Estrada
The "Versailles of Galicia" — a baroque estate with eight hectares of extraordinary gardens, granite waterways, and ancient camellias, owned by the Casa Ducal de Medinaceli Foundation. Open year-round. A natural complement to any wedding or celebration stay at the Pazo.
+34 986 58 74 35fundacionmedinaceli.org ↗
🌿 Gardens
Pazo de Santa Cruz de Ribadulla
~4 km · Santa Cruz de Ribadulla, Vedra
Considered the finest ornamental botanical garden in Galicia — 500-year-old olive trees, a naturalized camellia forest unique in Spain, ancient magnolias, and baroque stone fountains on a 30-hectare estate owned by the same family since the 16th century. Open year-round. Guided tours available led by the Marqués de Ribadulla himself.
+34 981 512 011pazoderivadulla.com ↗
🎣 River
Río Ulla · Cotos de Salmón
~4–12 km · A Estrada
One of Galicia's great salmon rivers runs virtually on the estate's doorstep. Five dedicated beats — Ximonde, Santeles, Sinde, Pontevea, and Couso — lie between 4 and 12 km away. Salmon and reo season runs May–July. From mid-June, artificial fly is the only permitted method on catch-and-release stretches — making this outstanding dry-fly water for brown trout and sea-run reo. We assist guests with permits and can connect you with local guides on request.
🎣 Salmon · Reo · TroutTurismo A Estrada ↗
🎣 River
Illas de Gres · Río Ulla
~8 km · Ledesma, Vila de Cruces
Five natural islands in the Río Ulla, connected by small footbridges and framed by ancient oak woodland — a scenic catch-and-release stretch on the same river as the estate's salmon beats. A beautiful and accessible spot, with a riverside recreational area and the medieval Ponte Ledesma nearby.
🎣 Catch & Releasegaliciamaxica.eu ↗

🥾 Hiking
Senda Ximonde–Pontevea · Río Ulla
~4 km · Ximonde
A beautiful easy riverside walk along the Río Ulla starting at Ximonde — one of Galicia's most celebrated salmon beats — and following the riverbank through ancient oak woodland to the medieval bridge of Pontevea. Waterfalls, old mills, and dramatic river scenery throughout.
🥾 4.5 km · Easyturismo.gal ↗
🥾 Hiking
Areal de Berres · Paseo Fluvial Ulla
~6 km · Berres, A Estrada
A magical 8.7 km riverside walk along the Río Ulla starting at O Areal de Berres — a unique recreation area filled with hand-carved stone sculptures created by local artist José Manuel Gañete. Ancient riparian forest, natural swimming in the river, and one of Galicia's most enchanting spots for the Midsummer solstice celebration.
🥾 8.7 km · EasyTrail guide ↗
🥾 Hiking
PR-G 136 · Monte Xesteiras
~10 km · A Estrada / Cuntis
A spectacular 14 km circular trail (PR-G 136) climbing Monte Xesteiras (716 m) through ancient cork oak groves, carballeiras, and muleteer paths. From the summit: panoramic views over the Rías Baixas, the Ulla estuary, Santiago de Compostela, and beyond. Also features unique stone formations, historic chapels, and restored water mills.
🥾 14 km · Moderate · 4 hrsTrail guide ↗
🥾 Hiking
Muíños de Golfariz · Pardemarín
~5 km · Pardemarín, A Estrada
A magical 3 km circular trail through the restored historic mills of Golfariz, lovingly signposted in carved stone by a local craftsman. Riparian forest, wooden walkways, and a series of beautifully preserved water mills make this one of A Estrada's most charming short walks — ideal for all ages.
🥾 3 km · EasyTrail guide ↗
🥾 Hiking
Fervenza de Callobre · Río Curantes
~5 km · Callobre, A Estrada
A short, enchanting circular trail (3.7 km) along the Río Curantes to the hidden Fervenza de Callobre — a dramatic 7-metre waterfall set in a magical valley of ancient mills, granite pools, and riparian woodland. Wooden walkways and bridges guide you through. One of A Estrada's best-kept secrets.
🥾 3.7 km · EasyTrail guide ↗
🥾 Hiking
Fervenza de Férveda · Silleda
~11 km · Escuadro, Silleda
A short and beautiful 2.7 km linear trail through ancient woodland to the spectacular Fervenza de Férveda. Along the way pass six historic waterwheels, a traditional washing place, and rich Atlantic forest. The last 200 metres descend steeply to the base of the waterfall — entirely worth it.
🥾 2.7 km · EasyTrail guide ↗
🥾 Hiking
Fervenza do Toxa · Silleda
~11 km · Silleda
The highest free-fall waterfall in Galicia — 30 metres — plunging through one of the most dramatic river gorges in the region. A well-marked 2.5 km path drops through chestnut, oak, and cork forest to the plunge pool. Can be extended to a full 12 km circular route taking in the medieval Monastery of Carboeiro and the river beach of A Carixa.
🥾 2.5 km · Easy (12 km full loop)Trail guide ↗
🥾 Hiking
PR-G 36 · Sendeiro de San Xoán da Cova
~5 km to start · Vedra
An 11.2 km linear riverside trail along the right bank of the Río Ulla from the Mirador de Gundián — with its iconic railway viaduct — downstream through ancient oak forest, the historic village of Ponte Ulla, and the five-millstone Muíño de Reboredo, finishing at the Ximonde salmon beat. One of the finest river walks in Galicia, practically on the estate's doorstep.
🥾 11.2 km · Medium · 3 hrs 15 minTrail guide ↗

🚴 MTB
Ruta BTT do Salmón · A Estrada
~9 km · A Estrada town
A Estrada's legendary annual MTB event route — now in its 26th year — organised by the local BikEstrada club. The 40 km circular circuit starts from the town, passes the Río Ulla salmon beats at Ximonde, crosses the iconic hanging bridge, and winds through forest tracks and Galician hill villages with 700 m of elevation. The GPS track from the 2019 edition is available on Wikiloc and rideable year-round.
🚴 38 km · MTB · Low–Intermediate · ~4 hrsWikiloc 2019 track ↗
🚴 Road Bike
Santa María Magdalena & Mirador de Gundián
~4 km · Riobó, A Estrada
A beautiful road cycling route starting from Riobó — just 4 km from the estate — climbing to the Iglesia de Santa María Magdalena and the spectacular Mirador de Gundián. The viewpoint looks directly down onto the dramatic Río Ulla gorge and the iconic 19th-century stone railway viaduct, one of the most photographed landmarks in Galicia. Quiet rural roads throughout.
🚴 Road Bike · Short–MediumWikiloc track ↗
🚴 MTB Centre
Centro BTT O Salnés · Meis
~37 km · Meis, O Salnés
Galicia's first and most popular official MTB centre, with 10 fully signposted routes for all levels radiating from the Campo de Golf de Meis. Routes range from a gentle 6.8 km family circuit to demanding 15 km mountain loops around Monte Castrove with panoramic views of the Rías Baixas. Bike hire, wash-down, showers, and tourism information on site.
🚴 6.8–15 km · 10 routes · All levelsturismo.gal ↗
