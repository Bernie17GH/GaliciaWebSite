Our Curated Experiences

🎣 River
Río Lérez · Coto de Cutián
~5 km · Campo Lameiro
One of Galicia's finest trout rivers, running through ancient oak forest virtually on the estate's doorstep. The Cutián beat offers 5 daily permits on a first-come basis. A guide is not included — but we can help you arrange one.
🎣 Trout · ReoPermits via DEPO ↗
🎣 River
Ríos Verdugo & Oitavén · Coto de Soutomaior
~12 km · Soutomaior
Two rivers meet at the spectacular Poza das Bestas beneath a historic hanging bridge — trout, barbel, and eel in pools beloved by local anglers for generations. Permits via the Xunta. We can help arrange a guide for the full experience.
🎣 Trout · BarbelSociedad de Pesca ↗
🎣 Coastal
Pescaturismo Cambados
~19 km · Cambados harbour
Shellfish harvesting experiences with the celebrated fisherwomen of Cambados — a deeply authentic, hands-on glimpse into Galician coastal culture.
★ 5.0+34 622 34 73 89pescaturismogalicia.com ↗
🎣 Coastal
Escaparía
~19 km · Sanxenxo marina
Private boat charters to hidden Atlantic beaches in the Aldan estuary. English-speaking captain Matt consistently earns raves — ideal for international guests.
★ 4.9+34 669 61 53 61escaparia.com ↗
🎣 Coastal
Barco La Toja
~24 km · Isla de La Toja
A beloved mussel boat experience — cruise the raft farms of O Grove with unlimited mussels and local wine. One of the most reviewed tourism experiences in the province.
★ 4.7+34 609 88 46 50barcolatoja.com ↗
🎣 Coastal
Cruceiros Rías Baixas
~24 km · O Grove
A large catamaran operation with 1,600+ reviews — the region's most popular group boat excursion. Mussels, paella, and local wine served on board with views of the estuary.
★ 4.6+34 986 73 13 43crucerosriasbaixas.com ↗
🎯 Game Hunting
Monteros Galaicos
~45 km · Tomiño & ~58 km · San Cristóbal de Cea, Ourense
A Galician hunting association organising driven big game hunts — wild boar, deer, and roe deer — through the oak and chestnut forests of the Ourense interior. A deeply traditional Galician field sport, available to visiting guests with the Pazo's assistance in arranging permits and logistics.
🎯 Big Game · Driven Hunts+34 618 78 42 82@monterosgalaicos ↗
🥾 Hiking
Sendero Litoral · Poio
~5 km · Poio
A 4.9 km easy coastal walk along the Ría de Pontevedra passing the Parque da Memoria, the oystercatcher estuary of Reiboa, ancient water mills, and the first oyster cultivation park on the Iberian peninsula. Superb birdwatching throughout.
🥾 4.9 km · 1h · Easysenderosazules.org ↗
🥾 Hiking
MountainPics Adventures
~6 km · Pontevedra city
Pablo leads extraordinary guided hiking and adventure expeditions across Galicia. A perfect fit for team-building excursions, nature wellness days, and multi-day itineraries.
★ 5.0+34 603 56 08 07mountainpics.es ↗
🥾 Hiking
Sendeiro Azul do Río Verdugo
~10 km · Ponte Caldelas
An 8–9 km circular trail hugging the Verdugo riverbank through ancient oak forests, stepping-stone crossings, old mills, and a river beach at A Calzada. The first inland trail in Spain to earn Blue Trail certification from ADEAC.
🥾 Blue Trail · All levelssenderosazules.org ↗
🥾 Hiking
Ruta Montes y Playas · Marín
~14 km · Marín
A spectacular 11 km route linking five Blue Flag beaches with ancient hilltop forest — and a visit to the extraordinary Bronze Age petroglyphs of Mogor, one of the finest rock art sites in Europe.
🥾 11 km · 3h · Easy–Mediumsenderosazules.org ↗
🥾 Hiking
Parque Natural del Río Barosa · PR-G 105
~15 km · Barro
A short but enchanting circular walk through a protected river park on the Portuguese Camino. Seventeen historic water mills line the trail as the Barosa river tumbles over a dramatic 30-metre waterfall. Family-friendly and walkable year-round.
🥾 3.2 km · 1h · Easyalltrails.com ↗
🥾 Hiking
Sendero Litoral Sanxenxo–Portonovo
~20 km · Sanxenxo
An 8.6 km coastal path between Sanxenxo and Portonovo tracing clifftop promenades, dune systems, and fishing village waterfronts — ending at the fish market lonja with views across the Ría.
🥾 8.6 km · 2h 30 · Easysenderosazules.org ↗
🥾 Hiking
Cascada de Segade · Río Umia
~20 km · Caldas de Reis
A riverside walk from the 19th-century botanical garden of Caldas de Reis to the spectacular Fervenza de Segade — a 30-metre waterfall with deep natural pools, perfect for swimming in summer.
🥾 6.8 km · 1h 30 · Moderatealltrails.com ↗
🥾 Hiking
Sendero Cabo Udra · Bueu
~25 km · Bueu
An 11.3 km circular trail through a Natura 2000 reserve at Cabo Udra — dramatic Atlantic headland, diverse habitats, and an on-site Nature Interpretation Centre. The most wild and challenging of the local Blue Trails.
🥾 11.3 km · 2h 45 · Mediumsenderosazules.org ↗
